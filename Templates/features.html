Tool Features


This tool was created to enable business owners and consumers to easily view and understand the sentiment associated with customer reviews from the popular review website Yelp. The Yelp reviews are extracted from the Yelp website and run through a logistic regression model that predicts the customer sentiment as either positive or negative. The logistic regression model was trained with over 200,000 unique Yelp reviews to ensure the highest level of accuracy possible.
